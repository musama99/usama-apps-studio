CricScore Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Usama Apps handles information when you use CricScore: Cricket Scoring.

Effective Date: July 2026Last Updated: July 2026Version 1.0.0
CricScore is an offline-first cricket scoring app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

1. Introduction

Thank you for using CricScore: Cricket Scoring, published by Usama Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how CricScore handles information when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

CricScore does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Payment information

Note: We do not collect or track device-specific information such as Android Advertising IDs, IMEI numbers, MAC addresses, or IP addresses.

3. Match Data

When you score cricket matches, the app may store match-related information locally on your device, including match names, team names, player names, scores, wickets, statistics, match history, and generated reports.

This data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

4. PDF Reports and Match Posters

CricScore allows you to generate PDF match reports and match posters. These files are created on your device. If you choose to share them, they are shared only through the apps or services you select.

CricScore does not upload your reports or posters to any external server.

5. App Permissions

CricScore may request storage or media-related permissions depending on your Android version. These permissions are used only for saving PDF reports, match posters, scorecard images, and sharing exported files through apps selected by you.

These permissions are not used to collect personal information.

6. Internet Usage

CricScore is designed to work offline. An internet connection is not required for scoring matches, viewing saved match history, generating scorecards, creating posters, or creating reports.

Third-Party Services

This version of the app does not contain advertisements or third-party tracking SDKs. If future versions introduce ad networks such as Google AdMob, this Privacy Policy will be updated and notice will be provided.

7. Data Sharing

CricScore does not sell user data, share user data with advertisers, upload match data to external servers, or share personal information with third parties.

Any sharing happens only when you intentionally choose to share a scorecard, poster, image, or PDF report through another app installed on your device.

8. Data Security

Match data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining access to your device and creating backups if needed.

Since CricScore does not upload your match data to a server, your local data remains under your control on your device.

9. Children's Privacy

CricScore does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not require accounts and does not collect personal information, no personal data from children is knowingly stored or shared by CricScore.

10. Data Deletion

Because CricScore stores match data locally in your device's isolated application storage, your data is deleted when you uninstall the app. You can also clear app data via Android Settings > Apps > CricScore > Storage > Clear Data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

12. Contact Us

13. Consent
