Questions

CricScore FAQ

Does CricScore require an account?

No. The current release is designed without account registration or sign-in.

Can I score matches without internet?

Yes. Core scoring, history, scorecards, posters, and report creation are designed for offline use.

Where is my match data stored?

Match information is stored locally in the app's storage on your device. Read the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I get support?

Visit the Support Center or email us with your device, Android version, and a description of the issue.
