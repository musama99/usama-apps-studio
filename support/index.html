Common questions

Support FAQ

Does CricScore work offline?

Yes. Core match setup, scoring, history, scorecards, posters, and report creation are designed to work without an internet connection.

Does CricScore require an account?

No. The current release does not require registration or sign-in.

How do I delete my match data?

Delete matches from the app where available, clear CricScore's storage in Android Settings, or uninstall the app. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

What should I include in a bug report?

Please include your app version, Android version, device model, exact steps, expected behavior, actual behavior, and screenshots or a screen recording when possible.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Support messages are reviewed as soon as practical. Response times may vary because Usama Apps Studio is independently operated.
